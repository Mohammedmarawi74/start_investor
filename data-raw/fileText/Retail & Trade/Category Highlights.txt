Consumer purchasing patterns, omnichannel shopping journeys, impulse buying, brand loyalty shifts, and the psychology of modern retail.

## Sports & Leisure

## Wholesale
Bulk distribution, B2B wholesale markets, distributor margins, and the wholesale channels that feed retail supply.
