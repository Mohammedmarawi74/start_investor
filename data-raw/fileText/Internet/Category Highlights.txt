# Internet

## Communications
Telecommunications infrastructure, internet penetration rates, broadband and mobile connectivity, and the evolution of digital communication platforms worldwide.

## Cyber Crime & Security
Cybersecurity threats, data breaches, ransomware attacks, and the growing market for security solutions. Includes government regulations and corporate security spending.

## Demographics & Use
Internet user demographics, online behavior patterns, device usage statistics, time spent online, and digital adoption across different age groups and regions.

## Mobile Internet & Apps
Smartphone-based internet usage, mobile app downloads, app revenue models, and the shift from desktop to mobile-first digital experiences.

## Online Search
Search engine market share, query volume trends, voice search adoption, and the role of AI-powered search algorithms in shaping information access.

## Online Video & Entertainment
Streaming platforms, video-on-demand, user-generated content, and the monetization of online entertainment including gaming streams and short-form video.

## Reach & Traffic
Website analytics, visitor metrics, bounce rates, conversion funnels, and the digital traffic patterns of major online properties and platforms.

## Social Media & User-Generated Content
Social network usage statistics, content creation trends, influencer marketing, platform engagement metrics, and the societal impact of social media.
