# Health, Pharma & Medtech

## Care & Support
Healthcare support services, home care, elderly care, and assistive services are growing rapidly as populations age globally. This sector includes in-home nursing, rehabilitation services, mental health support, and palliative care.

## Health Professionals & Hospitals
The backbone of any healthcare system — hospitals, clinics, and the medical professionals who staff them. Includes data on physician shortages, hospital bed capacity, specialist training, and healthcare workforce demographics.

## Health System
Overview of national healthcare systems, public vs. private healthcare spending, health insurance coverage rates, hospital admissions, and healthcare quality indicators across countries.

## Medical Technology
Medical devices, diagnostic equipment, imaging systems, and digital health technologies. This sector covers everything from surgical robots to wearable health monitors and AI-driven diagnostics.

## Pharmaceutical Products & Market
The global pharmaceutical market including drug development, generic vs. branded medications, vaccine production, regulatory approval pipelines, and revenue by therapeutic area.

## State of Health
Population health indicators including life expectancy, disease prevalence, vaccination rates, obesity, smoking, alcohol consumption, and other public health metrics worldwide.
