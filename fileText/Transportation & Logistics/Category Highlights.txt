# Transportation & Logistics

## Aviation
Commercial airline passenger numbers, cargo flight volumes, airport traffic, aircraft orders, and the post-pandemic recovery of air travel.

## Logistics
Supply chain management, freight forwarding, third-party logistics providers, warehouse automation, and the technology optimizing global goods movement.

## Public Transportation & Mobility Services
Bus ridership, metro systems, ride-sharing services, bike-sharing programs, and the future of urban mobility.

## Rail Transport
Passenger rail travel, freight rail volumes, high-speed rail expansion, and the role of railways in sustainable transportation.

## Vehicles & Road Traffic
Car ownership rates, traffic volumes, road safety statistics, electric vehicle adoption, and the infrastructure of road-based transportation.

## Water Transport
Shipping container volumes, port throughput, inland waterways, cruise passenger numbers, and the maritime trade network.
